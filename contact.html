Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days across India. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available for metro cities at ₹149 extra.

How do I return a product?

You can return any unused product within 30 days of delivery. Just contact us via email or phone and we'll arrange a pickup at no extra charge.

Do you offer bulk / wholesale pricing?

Yes! We have special pricing for schools, sports academies, and bulk buyers. Contact us at bulk@sipahisports.com for a custom quote.

Are your products genuinely certified?

Absolutely. All Sipahi Sports products are 100% genuine, manufactured to ISI/BIS or equivalent international standards. We carry a 2-year warranty on most products.
