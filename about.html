Our Story

Sipahi Sports was founded in 2005 by Rajiv Kumar, a former Ranji Trophy cricketer who noticed a glaring gap: the best sports equipment was either too expensive or too hard to find for grassroots athletes across India.

Starting from a small shop in Andheri, Mumbai, we've grown into one of India's leading sports equipment brands — trusted by school sports teams, state-level athletes, and weekend warriors alike.

Today, Sipahi Sports ships to all 28 states, has over 5 lakh happy customers, and offers more than 500 products across cricket, football, badminton, fitness, and more.
